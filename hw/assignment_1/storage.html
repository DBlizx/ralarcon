Storage

A HDD (hard disk drive) will store your permanent data. This is where you can store pictures and documents. The HDD will save or write and output or read your data. An SSD (solid state drive) is faster in reading and writing data. SSDs are smaller, quieter, and lighter, since they have no mechanical parts. Though, SSDs are more expensinve.

If you're a college student, it is recommended to get a laptop with an SSD. Check below for the speed difference between an HDD and SSD.
