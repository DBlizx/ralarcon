The GPU

If you are going to work with 3-D applications and graphics, such as in Photoshop and playing the newest games, you will need a good GPU. Without a GPU, the CPU would be put in a lot of strain and the computer might "crash". GPU's (graphics processing unit) processes graphics...derr. Would you need a GPU for Minesweeper? No. But you will for Grand Theft Auto 5. Newer games take more processing power. Something like Blender will also need a fast GPU to process 3-D modeling.
